A companion, not a teacher

Built around personal practice

Breathing is deeply personal. Many people already have practices they trust — from pranayama traditions, yoga teachers, clinicians, or their own lived experience.

PraniQ respects that. We do not tell you how to breathe, nor do we replace your teacher. We simply help you hold the practice you already have, offering a quiet space to practise with consistency, clarity, and calm.

And if you are just beginning, it meets you there too. Starting from your intention, your physical comfort, and your breathing capacity, PraniQ suggests a simple sequence you can follow — and shape over time into your own practice.

Shaped by classic learning and modern understanding, PraniQ is a respectful bridge between the old and the useful — freely available to everyone.

Why this exists

"Something as fundamental as breath should be supported — not sold."

PranaFlow began as something personal. After losing a close family member to breathing-related illness, and seeing how deeply breath shapes recovery, wellbeing, and everyday life, one idea stayed simple:

It should be accessible.

So PraniQ was built to be free — and always will be.
